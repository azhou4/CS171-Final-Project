The visualization above is a sankey diagram that depicts the income percentiles of different races in America. The width of the connections represents a flow rate, and is a proportion of the entire population. The origination nodes represent the races, while the destination nodes represent the income percentile buckets.


America's wealth gap is enlarging, with a large amount of wealth unequally distributed in the Top 1%.
